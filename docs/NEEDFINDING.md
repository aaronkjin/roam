# Needfinding Interview Script

## Focus

Travel planning habits, booking processes, pain points, decision-making, tools used

# Preparation Steps

1. Notetaker: start the recording and enable the closed captions
   a. Pay attention to long pauses, change in tone, emotions while talking about last
   purchase, any trouble recalling purchases?
2. Interviewer: confirm that the recording is started before the interview

## Script

Introduction: Hi, my name is **\_\_** and we are conducting user research on people’s
experiences with booking travel. This interview is designed to help us gain a deeper
understanding of how you plan and book trips.
We would like to record this Zoom meeting for audio transcription and notetaking purposes,
would you be comfortable with that? The recording and all your responses will be
confidential, of course.

## Questions

Background and Context
● What kind of trips do you typically take? How often?
● Do you typically travel alone or with others? Who do you usually travel with?
Grand Tour
● Can you walk me through the last time you planned and booked a trip?
○ Where were you going and why?
○ Who were you traveling with?
○ Where were you when you started planning (physically and mentally - at
home, at work, stressed, excited?)
○ What was the first thing you did when you decided to take this trip?
○ What did you book first? Why that order?
○ What websites, apps, or tools did you use during the planning process?

■ Why did you choose these tools?
■ Did you switch between different platforms?
○ How long did the entire booking and planning process take from start to
finish?
Decision Making and Research
● How did you decide on your destination?
● How did you research your options for flights/accommodations/activities?
● Did you read reviews? If so, where and how many?
● Did you compare prices across different platforms? How many?
● What factors were most important in your booking decisions?
● Were there any moments you felt stuck or unsure about what to do next?
Pain Points
● What was the most challenging or frustrating part of planning this trip?
● Were there any surprises or unexpected issues during the booking process?
● Did you feel overwhelmed at any point? What caused that feeling?
Post Booking
● After you booked everything, how did you organize or keep track of your trip details?
● Did you make any changes to your bookings after the initial purchase
● How did you share trip information with travel companions
Group Travel
● If traveling with others, how did you coordinate with your travel companions during
planning?
● How did you make group decisions about destinations, accommodations, and activities?
● What tools or methods did you use to share/communicate information?
● What was most challenging about planning for a group?
Booking Order/Flexibility
● Do you always book things in the same order? Why or why not?
● How far in advance do you typically book different components of your trip?
● Do you prefer booking everything at once or spacing it out? Why?
Information Management
● How do you keep track of confirmations, reservations, and other important trip details?
● Do you create itineraries? If so, how detailed?
● What information do you find yourself repeatedly looking up or checking

Budget and Price
● How do you track your budget while planning?
● Do you use price alerts or tracking tools? How much time do you spend looking for details or
comparing prices?
● How much time do you spend looking for deals or comparing prices?
Past Experiences
● Can you tell me about a particularly smooth or easy trip planning experience? What made it
that way?
● Can you tell me about a particularly difficult or frustrating planning experience? What went
wrong?
Inspiration and Discovery
● How do you discover new destinations or activities?
● What inspires you to take trips?
● How do you decide what to do at your destination?
